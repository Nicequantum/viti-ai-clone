### WHEN VOICE ISN'T WORKING

| # | Problem | Fix |
|---|---------|-----|
| 1 | Mic button missing | Type manually · tell Service Manager |
| 2 | "Microphone blocked" | Settings → Allow mic → reload |
| 3 | Mic does nothing | Use **Chrome or Edge** |
| 4 | Words cut off | **Push-to-talk** · tap Retry |
| 5 | Wrong words | **Edit the text** — normal fix |
| 6 | Timeout / stopped alone | Tap **Retry** · shorter chunks |

**Still stuck?** TYPE YOUR NOTES · fix the car first

---

### NOISY BAY TIPS
- **Push-to-talk** near lifts, tools, compressors
- Hold tablet **6–12 inches** from your mouth
- Face **away** from loudest noise
- Noise meter says *Very noisy*? → push-to-talk or move
- Short bursts: finding · test · repair
