## What is Merlin?

**Merlin** is Mercedes-Benz's shop-floor warranty story platform. It helps service technicians document repair findings and produce professional, submission-ready warranty narratives — faster and more consistently than manual typing alone.

Technicians document **what they actually tested and repaired**. Merlin formats that work into clear Complaint–Cause–Correction stories for CDK and warranty submission.

---

## Why we are going live now

| Challenge today | Merlin's answer |
|-----------------|-----------------|
| Technicians type long stories at the end of the day | **Hands-free voice input** — speak notes while working on the vehicle |
| Story quality varies by technician | AI-assisted drafts aligned to MI 2.0 standards — always reviewed before submit |
| Warranty rework and chargebacks from unclear documentation | Structured 3 C's narratives with audit trail for accountability |
| No visibility into who generated what | Tamper-evident audit log for service leadership |

---

## The technician experience — voice first

The **single biggest daily benefit** for technicians is **hands-free voice input** on shop-floor tablets:

- **Tap to toggle** — tap the mic, speak your findings, tap to stop
- **Push-to-talk** — hold the mic in noisy bays near lifts and compressors
- **Manual typing always available** — no job is blocked if voice or AI is temporarily unavailable

Technicians stay at the vehicle. Merlin captures their words and turns them into a story draft they review and edit before submission.

## What leadership should know

### Merlin strengthens — it does not replace — technician judgment

- AI **does not invent** test results. Undocumented work appears as `[NOT DOCUMENTED]`.
- Every story is **reviewed and edited** by the technician before CDK submission.
- Sensitive customer data is **encrypted** at rest; sessions revoke instantly on password change.
